不

HSK Level: 1

HELLO THERE!
我 不 吃 冰淇淋 。
wǒ bù chī bīngqílín 。
I don't eat ice-cream.

不 serves as a negator in Chinese. We can put it before a verb, an adjective, an adverb or a preposition. Sentences with 不 are used to describe a present, future or habitual event, unless a specific time in the past is stated.

不 to negate verbs

We can use 不 to negate an action verb, stative verb or a modal verb.
不 + verb

我 不 吃 冰淇淋 。
wǒ bù chī bīngqílín 。
I don't eat ice-cream.
我 不 是 老師 。
wǒ bù shì lǎoshī 。
I am not a teacher.
我 不 會 原諒 你 。
wǒ bù huì yuánliàng nǐ 。
I will not forgive you.

Note that we use another negator 沒 to negate the verb 有. For example:

我 沒 有 錢 。
wǒ méi yǒu qián 。
I don't have money.
我 沒 有 孩子 。
wǒ méi yǒu háizi 。
I don't have children.

To learn more about the usage of 有, click here.

不 to negate adjectives

不 + adjective

我的 房子 不 大 。
wǒ de fángzi bù dà 。
My house is not big.
他 不 帥 ， 但 很 聰明 。
tā bù shuài ， dàn hěn cōngmíng 。
He is not handsome but very smart.
這 孩子 不 怪 ， 你 才 怪 。
zhè háizi bù guài ， nǐ cái guài 。
This child is not weird, you are the weird one.

不 to negate adverbs

Most of the time we put adverbs before 不 (bù), but there are a few adverbs that can occur before 不 as well. For example:
不 + adverb

我 不 常 去 圖書館 。
wǒ bù cháng qù túshūguǎn 。
I don't go to the library often.
你 不 一定 會 贏 。
nǐ bù yīdìng huì yíng 。
You may not win.

不 to negate adverbs

不 + preposition

我 今天 不 在 家 吃飯 。
wǒ jīntiān bù zài jiā chīfàn 。
I am not going to eat at home today.
他 現在 不 跟 我 說話 了 。
tā xiànzài bù gēn wǒ shuōhuà le 。
He doesn't talk to me now.
他 不 與 其他 人 互動 ， 只 自己 坐 在 那兒 。
tā bù yǔ qítā rén hùdòng ， zhǐ zìjǐ zuò zài nàr 。
He doesn't interact with others, but just sitting there alone.
