午安

HSK Level: 2

午安 (wǔān) means good afternoon. Unlike 早上好 (zǎoshang hǎo) or 晚安 (wǎnān) , it is not very commonly used in written or spoken form. We usually use 你好 (nǐhǎo) instead. Regardless, we will look at some examples with 午安 (wǔān) here.

午安 ， 你 睡 得 好 嗎 ？
wǔān ， nǐ shuì de hǎo ma ？
Good afternoon, did you sleep well?
午安 ， 飯 煮 好 了 。
wǔān ， fàn zhǔ hǎo le 。
Good afternoon, meals are ready.
午安 ， 您 是 劉太太 嗎 ？
wǔān ， nín shì liú tàitai ma ？
Good afternoon, are you Mrs Liu?
