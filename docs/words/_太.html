太

HSK Level: 1

太 (tài) means too, as in too much.
When used in a sentence, you will usually add a 了 (le) at the end of the sentence as well:

這 食物 太 辣 了 ！
zhè shíwù tài là le !
This food is too spicy!
我 不能 做 ， 太 難 了 。
wǒ bùnéng zuò , tài nán le .
I can't do it. It's too difficult.
太 is also used colloquially with a meaning similar to very. (Just like in English!)
小狗 太 可愛 了 ！
xiǎogǒu tài kěài le !
The puppy is too cute!
