週末

HSK Level: 3

週末 (zhōumò) means weekend. It is made up of the words 週 (zhōu), which means week and 末 (mò), which means end.

你 週末 做 什麼 ？
nǐ zhōumò zuò shénme ？
What are you doing this weekend?
派對 會 在 下 個 週末 舉行 。
pàiduì huì zài xià gè zhōumò jǔxíng 。
The party will be in the next weekend.
我們 上 個 週末 在 泰國 。
wǒmen shàng gè zhōumò zài tàiguó 。
We were in Thailand last weekend.
