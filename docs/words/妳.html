妳

HSK Level: 1

妳 means "you" in informal occasions and is used only for females. Note the female radical 女 on the left side of the character. The male counterpart is 你 and the formal version is 您.

妳 喜歡 吃 壽司 嗎 ？
Nǐ xǐhuān chī shòusī ma?
Do you like to eat sushi?
他 在 追 妳 。
Tā zài zhuī nǐ.
He is pursuing you.

Like all pronouns, 們 will make it plural. 妳們 means "they (plural-female)" or "them (plural-female)".

妳們 是 新加坡人 嗎 ？
Nǐmen shì xīnjiāpōrén ma?
Are you Singaporean?
我 會 幫助 妳們 。
Wǒ huì bāngzhù nǐmen.
I will help you.
