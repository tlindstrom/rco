明天

HSK Level: 1

明天 (míngtiān) means tomorrow. It is made up of 明 (míng), which means next or bright, and 天 (tiān), the word for day.

明天 會 發生 什麼 事 ？
míngtiān huì fāshēng shénme shì ？
What will happen tomorrow?
明天 見 ！
míngtiān jiàn ！
See you tomorrow!
我們 明天 會 煮 咖哩 。
wǒmen míngtiān huì zhǔ kāli 。
We are going to cook curry tomorrow.
