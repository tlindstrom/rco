姓氏

HSK Level: 2

姓氏 (xìngshì) means surname.

你的 姓氏 是 什麼 ？
nǐ de xìngshì shì shénme ？
What is your surname?
中國 最 普遍 的 姓氏 是 什麼 ？
zhōngguó zuì pǔbiàn de xìngshì shì shénme ？
What is the most common surname in China?

Sometimes we just use 姓 (xìng) to express surname. However, when 姓 (xìng) stands alone without 氏 (shì), it has an additional function: to function as 'to be surnamed'. The following is some examples:

「 劉 」 是 大 姓 。
「 liú 」 shì dà xìng 。
Liu is a common surname.
你 姓 什麼 ？
nǐ xìng shénme ？
What is your surname? (Lit: What are you surnamed?)
我 姓 王 。
wǒ xìng wáng 。
My surname is Wang.(Lit: I am surnamed Wang)

(Note that to tell people what our surname is, we usually answer 我姓... instead of 我的姓氏是...)
The Chinese word for full name is 姓名 (xìngmíng), which is comprised of the 名 (míng) in 名字 (míngzi) , which means name, and the 姓 (xìng) in 姓氏 (xìngshì).
