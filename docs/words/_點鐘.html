點鐘

點鐘 (diǎn zhōng) means o'clock.

現在 幾 點鐘 了 ？ 還 在 睡 ！
xiànzài jǐ diǎnzhōng le ？ hái zài shuì ！
What time is it now? You are still sleeping!
兩 點鐘 見 ！
liǎng diǎnzhōng jiàn ！
See you at 2 o'clock!
我 五 點鐘 下 班 。
wǒ wǔ diǎnzhōng xià bān 。
I am off at 5 o'clock.

We can also use the short form 點 (diǎn) as 點鐘 (diǎn zhōng) like in the following:

我 十 點 睡覺 。
wǒ shí diǎn shuìjué 。
I go to bed at 10 o'clock.
我 七 點 起床 。
wǒ qī diǎn qǐchuáng 。
I get up at 7 o'clock.
