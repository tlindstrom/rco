比

HSK Level: 2

We use 比 (bǐ) to make comparisons. Here, we will break its function down and see how exactly we can use this word to make comparisons.

比 as to compare

沒 有 東西 可以 跟 點心 比 。
méi yǒu dōngxi kěyǐ gēn diǎnxīn bǐ 。
Nothing can compare with the dimsums.
放棄 吧 ， 你 根本不 能 跟 她 比 。
fàngqì ba ， nǐ gēnběnbù néng gēn tā bǐ 。
Just give up, you can't compare with her at all.
你 要 比 我 高 還是 你 高 ？
nǐ yào bǐ wǒ gāo háishì nǐ gāo ？
Do you want to compare whether I am taller or you are taller?

比 as a comparative preposition

When we want to compare A and B, we often use the following structure:
A + 比 + B + adjective
Both A and B can be a phrase or a clause but we usually want to keep B to the minimum, which is to only have the element being compared with A. Let's look at some examples:

中國 比 日本 大 。
zhōngguó bǐ rìběn dà 。
China is larger than Japan.
我 喝 的 白蘭地 比 你的 烈 。
wǒ hē de báilándì bǐ nǐ liè 。
The brandy I drank was stronger than the one you drank.
我 跑 得 比 你 快 。
wǒ pǎo de bǐ nǐ kuài 。
I run faster than you.
我 看 得 比 你 遠 。
wǒ kàn de bǐ nǐ yuǎn 。
I can see further than you.

When we are comparing the subjects of the same action, just like the last 2 examples in the above, we can also reorder the constituents like in the following so the subjects to be compared can share the same verb:

我 比 你 跑 得 快 。
wǒ bǐ nǐ pǎo de kuài 。
I run faster than you.
我 比 你 看 得 遠 。
wǒ bǐ nǐ kàn de yuǎn 。
I can see further than you.

Of course, we can still keep the repetitive elements but it could sound redundant. For example, we can rewrite the two examples in the above like in the following:

我 跑 得 比 你 跑 得 快 。
wǒ pǎo de bǐ nǐ pǎo de kuài 。
I run faster than you.
我 看 得 比 你 看 得 遠 。
wǒ kàn de bǐ nǐ kàn de yuǎn 。
I can see further than you.

Also, we can complement the adjective in the comparative structure so we can provide extra information about how much is more or less, etc., just like the examples below:

我的 茶 比 你的 苦 多 了 。
wǒ de chá bǐ nǐ de kǔ duō le 。
My tea is way bitter than yours.
我 只 是 比 你 大 一些 。
wǒ zhǐ shì bǐ nǐ dà yīxiē 。
I am only a little bit older than you.
我的 租金 比 你的 貴 三百 塊 。
wǒ de zūjīn bǐ nǐ de guì sānbǎi kuài 。
My rent is more expensive than yours by 300 bucks.

比 as to used in scores or ratios

比 can be translated as to when we want to compare scores in a match or when we talk about ratios.
For example:

德國 以 七 比 一 打敗 巴西 。
déguó yǐ qī bǐ yī dǎbài bāxī 。
Germany beat Brazil by 7 to 1.
你 覺得 會 幾 比 幾 呢 ？
nǐ juéde huì jǐ bǐ jǐ ne ？
What do think the score will be?
蔬菜 與 肉 的 比例 應該 大概 三 比 一 。
shūcài yǔ ròu de bǐlì yīnggāi dàgài sān bǐ yī 。
The vegetable to meat consumption ratio should be around 3 to 1.

比 as ratio

最 完美 的 「 米 水 」 比 是 多少 呢 ？
zuì wánměi de 「 mǐ shuǐ 」 bǐ shì duōshǎo ne ？
What is the perfect ratio of rice to water?
二 與 四 的 比 等於 一 與 二 的 比 。
èr yǔ sì de bǐ děngyú yī yǔ èr de bǐ 。
The ratio of 2 to 4 equals to that of 1 to 2.
