做

HSK Level: 1

做 (zuò) is a very versatile word which can be translated into various English words. It means to bring something into existence; it is also an equivalent of to do or to be/become.

做 as to bring something into existence

做 (zuò) is often translated into to make, to produce or to create , etc. We should be aware that they are not the only possible translations, 做 (zuò) can also be used for action verbs that denote bringing something into existence, and the 'something' could be abstract, such as a plan, or concrete like a cake. 做 (zuò) is put between a subject and the thing that is brought into existence:
subject + 做 + object brought to existence
For example:

我 做 了 一 個 重大 的 決定 。
wǒ zuò le yī ge zhòngdà de juédìng 。
I have made an important decision.
這 公司 做 了 大量 有毒 的 產品 。
zhè gōngsī zuò le dàliáng yǒudú de chǎnpǐn 。
This company produced a lot of toxic products.
我 昨晚 做 了 一 個 惡夢 。
wǒ zuówǎn zuò le yī ge èmèng 。
I had a nightmare last night.
我們 喜歡 自己 做 麵包 。
wǒmen xǐhuān zìjǐ zuò miànbāo 。
We love to make our own breads.
我們 做 了 一 條 新 規則 。
wǒmen zuò le yī tiáo xīn guīzé 。
We have created a new rule.

做 as to do

When we perform or engage in something, we can use 做 (zuò)as the verb. It is used in the following sentence structure:
subject + 做 + activity to be performed or engaged in
For example:

我們 做 生意 要 用 腦 。
wǒmen zuò shēngyì yào yòng nǎo 。
We need to be smart to do business.
我 不 要 做 苦工 。
wǒ bù yào zuò kǔgōng 。
I don't want to do labor work.
你 在 做 什麼 ？
nǐ zài zuò shénme ？
What are you doing?
別 做 犯法 的 東西 ， 好嗎 ？
bié zuò fànfǎ de dōngxi ， hǎoma ？
Don't do illegal things, ok?

做 as to be or to become

做 (zuò) could be a Chinese equivalent of to be or to become. Similar to the English sentence structure, we simply put 做 (zuò) between its subject and object.
subject + 做 + object
For example:

我們 做 了 朋友 。
wǒmen zuò le péngyǒu 。
We have become friends.
我 要 做 總統 。
wǒ yào zuò zǒngtǒng 。
I want to become a president.
這 沙發 可以 做 我的 床 。
zhè shāfā kěyǐ zuò wǒ de chuáng 。
This sofa can be my bed.
我 做 了 父母 兩 年 了 。
wǒ zuò le fùmǔ liǎng nián le 。
I have been a parent since two years ago.
