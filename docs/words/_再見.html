再見

HSK Level: 1

再見 (zàijiàn) means goodbye.

我 要 走 了 ， 再見 ！
wǒ yào zǒu le ， zàijiàn ！
I need to go now, goodbye!
再見 ， 保重 ！
zàijiàn ， bǎozhòng ！
Bye, take care!

再見 (zàijiàn) literally means to see again. Sometimes it appears in a sentence and functions as a verb phrase like in the following:

我 什麼 時候 會 再 見 你 ？
wǒ shénme shíhòu huì zài jiàn nǐ ？
When will I see you again?
我 不 能 再 見 你 了 。
wǒ bù néng zài jiàn nǐ le 。
I can't see you anymore.

In a more casual occasion, we also use the loanword 拜拜 (bàibai) to say goodbye:

今晚 見 ， 拜拜 ！
jīnwǎn jiàn ， bàibai ！
See you tonight, bye!
