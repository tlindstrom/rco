號碼

號碼 (hàomǎ) means number. Unlike 數字 (shùzì), which also means number, it particular means number that is assigned to something, for example, the 'number' in 'street number' is assigned to a street and therefore, we use 街道號碼 (jiēdào hàomǎ) instead of 街道數字 (jiēdào shùzì). Let's have a look at some examples and you will grasp a better understanding of the usage of 號碼 (hàomǎ).

你的 手機 號碼 是 多少 ？
nǐde shǒujī hàomǎ shì duōshǎo ？
What is your mobile phone number?
一 個 個人 車牌 號碼 可以 很 貴 。
yī ge gerén chēpái hàomǎ kěyǐ hěn guì 。
A personalized license plate could be costly.

When a number is not assigned to anything, we usually use 數字 (shùzì). The following is some examples:

密碼 必須 包括 字母 和 數字 。
mìmǎ bìxū bāokuò yīngwén zìmǔ hé shùzì 。
The password must contain letters and numbers.
「 四 」 是 個 不 吉利 的 數字 。
「 sì 」 shì ge bù jílì de shùzì 。
'Four' is an unlucky number.
