  3. The Reduplication of Verbs
In Chinese, a verb can be duplicated to express the meaning of ‘briefly’, to trivialize actions or to soften speech tones when used in a request/command. We can duplicate monosyllabic or disyllabic verbs, and their structures are used differently.
Let's have a look at the reduplication of monosyllabic verbs first and then we will go into detail of that of disyllabic verbs.

Monosyllabic verbs ##

Monosyllabic verbs can be reduplicated these ways:
AA or A一A
Let's have some examples now:
你看看這本書。
nǐ kànkan zhè běn shū 。
Have a look at this book.
等等我！
děngděng wǒ ！
Wait for me!
我要坐一坐。
wǒ yào zuòyīzuò 。
I need to sit down for a moment.
說說笑而已，別那麼認真！
shuōshuoxiào éryǐ ， bié nàmó rènzhēn ！
I was just joking, don't be so serious!
我會想一想。
wǒ huì xiǎngyīxiǎng 。
I will think about it.
我只是叫你試一試它，不是叫你把它吃光！
wǒ zhǐshì jiào nǐ shìyīshì tā ， bùshì jiào nǐ bǎ tā chīguāng ！
I told you to try a small bite, not to eat the whole thing!
我只是親一親她，沒什麼大不了！
wǒ zhīshì qīnyīqīn tā ， méishénmedàbùliǎo ！
I only kissed her, no big deal!

Disyllabic verbs ##

Disyllabic verbs are duplicated in the following way:
ABAB
For example:
你累了，去休息休息吧！
nǐ léi le ， qù xiūxixiūxi ba ！
You are tired. Go take a rest!
你能打掃打掃這個房子嗎？
nǐ néng dǎsǎodǎsǎo zhè ge fángzǐ ma ？
Can you clean up the house?
我要跟你學習學習。
wǒ yào gēn nǐ xuéxíxuéxí 。
I need to learn from you.
去打聽打聽他的背景。
qù dǎtīngdǎtīng tā bèijǐng 。
Go make inquiries about his background.
Please note that not all verbs can be duplicated and there is no clear rule on which verbs can be duplicated. Reduplication of mono-syllabic verbs is more common than reduplication of disyllabic verbs.
The following are some incorrect examples of verbs we should avoid reduplicating:
你只需答應答應我！
nǐ zhǐ xū dāyìngydāyìng wǒ ！
我會恨恨你。
wǒ huì hènhèn nǐ 。
我只是拒絕拒絕了他。
wǒ zhīshì jùjuéjùjué le tā 。
我打破打破了你的碗。
wǒ dǎpòdǎpò le nǐ wǎn 。

Practice ##

Now it's your turn! How would you reduplicate the verbs in the following sentences?
我去了打球而已。
wǒ qù le dǎqiú éryǐ 。
I only went for a ballgame.
您考慮我的提議吧！
nín kǎolǜ wǒde tíyì ba ！
Please consider my suggestion!
