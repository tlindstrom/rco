Welcome to Read Chinese Online, the website that makes practicing reading Chinese easy!
This article explains how the site works, and how to use it to learn Chinese. If you're new to the website, this is a great place to start.

How to use this website

Notice how at the top of the screen there are buttons saying TRADITIONAL, SIMPLIFIED and PINYIN. Using these buttons you can choose how you want the Chinese writing to appear on the site.
You choose whether you want to see traditional or simplified characters, and if you click the PINYIN button, you will turn on pinyin mode, where Chinese characters are replaced by phonetic text (pinyin). This is awesome for practicing your understanding without having to learn all the characters to begin with!
You can try out the different modes right now. Try clicking the buttons, and see how the example sentence below changes in accordance with what you choose:
歡迎來到我們的網站！
huānyínglái dào wǒmén de wǎngzhàn ！
Welcome to our website!
Notice how example sentences are interactive. If you hover your mouse pointer over a word (or touch it, on a phone or tablet), a box will pop up with the pronunciation and meaning of this word.
Now, try right clicking a word! A context menu will pop up, with two options: Listen to word and Listen to phrase. Click these to have the computer read out the word, or the entire phrase, for you, so you know how it is pronounced.
Also, notice how some words have a little book icon in the corner of the box that pops up? This means they are linked to a word page, which you can reach from the right-click menu. This is an article about this particular word, containing explanations of common uses, important grammar, and of course, more example sentences!

Parallel Texts

In addition to lesson style articles like this one, our website also contains a huge library of parallel texts, which look like this:
這是一個中英對照的文章。
zhè shì yī ge zhōngyīngduìzhào de wénzhāng 。
This is a parallel text.
中英對照的文章有效幫助我們練習閱讀中文。
zhōngyīngduìzhào de wénzhāng yǒuxiào bāngzhù wǒmén liànxí yuèdú zhōngwén 。
Parallel texts are great for practicing reading Chinese.
希望你們會享受與我們一起學習漢語！
xīwàng nǐmén huì xiǎngshòu yǔ wǒmén yīqǐ xuéxí hànyǔ ！
We hope you will enjoy learning Chinese with us!
These texts show a Chinese text on the left, and its English translation to the right. Hovering your mouse pointer over a sentence will light it up on both sides, making it know what any sentence means, and to keep track of where in the text you are.
The parallel texts also possess the same functionalities as the example sentences. You can hover your mouse pointer over words to see information, you can have the computer read out pronunciations, and many words are linked to example sentences (look for the book icon!)

Usage Tips

  • Although we strive to make our content work well on all platforms, we recommend viewing RCO on a desktop computer, since interacting with the pages using a mouse cursor provides the superior learning experience.
  • The quality of the voice when listening to words and sentences is dependent on your web browser and operating system. If you don't like the sound of the generated voice, or you don't hear anything at all, you can try another web browser, or another computer.
