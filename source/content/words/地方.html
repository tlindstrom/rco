地方 (dìfang) is a noun which means place, room or space, or part.

地方 as place, room or space ##

你 最後 選 了 哪 一 個 地方 ？
nǐ zuìhòu xuǎn le nǎ yī gè dìfang ？
Which place did you choose in the end?
這 還 有 進步 的 地方 。
zhè hái yǒu jìnbù de dìfang 。
There is still room for improvement.
我們 沒 有 什麼 地方 養 寵物 。
wǒmen méi yǒu shénme dìfang yǎng chǒngwù 。
We don't have much space for a pet.

地方 as part##

地方 (dìfang) can also refers to part of something.
這 建議書 有 可取 的 地方 。
zhè jiànyìshū yǒu kěqǔ de dìfang 。
Some parts of the proposal have merits.
這 文章 有些 地方 錯 了 。
zhè wénzhāng yǒuxiē dìfang cuò le 。
There are some mistakes in the article.
When 地方 is pronounced as dìfāng, it is used to describe a place that is administered by a regional authority. The possible translations are regional or local. Here are some examples:
地方 政府 可以 十分 腐敗 。
dìfāng zhèngfǔ kěyǐ shífēn fǔbài 。
Regional authorities could be very corrupt.
有些 地方 組織 在 抗議 提高 稅 率 。
yǒuxiē dìfāng zǔzhī zài kàngyì tígāo shuì lǜ 。
Some local organisations are protesting against the increase of tax rate.
