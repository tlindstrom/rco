國家 (guó jiā) means country and the classifier for it is 個 (ge).

你 去 過 多少 個 國家 ？
nǐ qù guò duōshǎo ge guójiā ？
How many countries have you been to?
世界 上 最 美 的 國家 是 哪 一 個 ？
shìjiè shàng zuì měi de guójiā shì nǎ yī ge ？
Which is the most beautiful country in the world?
我的 國家 比 你的 國家 大 。
wǒ de guójiā bǐ nǐ de guójiā dà 。
My country is bigger than your country.

家 (jiā), which means family or home, forms part of the word 國家 (guó jiā). From this we can see a close relationship between family and country. In Chinese culture, it has been often said that '國家國家，國就是我們的家 (guójiā guójiā , guó jiùshì wǒmen de jiā)', which implies that we should love and be loyal to our country.
