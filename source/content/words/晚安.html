晚安 (wǎnān) means good night. Here are some examples with 晚安 (wǎnān):

我 要 睡 了 ， 晚安 。
wǒ yào shuì le ， wǎnān 。
I am going to bed, good night.
明天 再 說 ， 晚安 。
míngtiān zài shuō ， wǎnān 。
Talk to you tomorrow, good night.

Some young people like to say wǎnān to their lovers because it is like an acronym of 我愛你愛你 (wǒàinǐàinǐ). So it actually kills two birds with one stone, isn't it practical?
