妳 means you in informal occasions. The formal version is 您.

妳 is used only for females. Note the female radical 女 on the left side of the character. The male counterpart is 你.

妳 喜歡 吃 壽司 嗎 ？
Nǐ xǐhuān chī shòusī ma?
Do you like to eat sushi?
他 在 追 妳 。
Tā zài zhuī nǐ.
He is pursuing you.

Like all pronouns, 們 will make it plural. 妳們 means they (plural-female) or them (plural-female).

妳們 是 新加坡人 嗎 ？
Nǐmen shì xīnjiāpōrén ma?
Are you Singaporean?
我 會 幫助 妳們 。
wǒ huì bāngzhù nǐmen.
I will help you.
