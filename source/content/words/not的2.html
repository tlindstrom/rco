的 is a function word primarily used to express possession, and to describe things. Grammatically speaking, what 的 does is to attach a modifying clause to a noun.

Usage

A common usage of 的 is to describe who owns something, similarly to how of and genitive 's work in English.

媽媽 的 衣服 。
Māma de yīfú.
Mom's clothes.
那 個 男孩 的 狗 。
Nà ge nánhái de gǒu.
That boy's dog.

It is also used to connect an adjective to the noun it describes:

白色 的 沙 。
Báisè de shā.
White sand.
