沒關係 (méi guānxi) can be translated into it doesn't matter or to have no relations.

沒關係 as it doesn't matter ##

We use 沒關係 (méi guānxi) when someone has apologized to us and we think it is fine.
Let's have a look at some examples:
你打破了我的花瓶？沒關係，我會買一個新的。
nǐ dǎpò le wǒ de huāpíng ？ méiguānxi ， wǒ huì mǎi yī gè xīn de 。
You broke my vase? It doesn't matter, I will buy a new one.
沒關係，下次不要遲了。
méiguānxi ， xià cì bùyào chí le 。
It doesn't matter, just don't be late next time.
We also use 沒關係 (méi guānxi) in the sense of it doesn't matter when we consider something not important. It has the following sentence structure:
topic that is not important + 沒關係
他來不來也沒關係。
tā lái bù lái yě méiguānxi 。
Whether he comes or not doesn't matter to me.
我明天穿拖鞋沒關係吧？
wǒ míngtiān chuān tuōxié méiguānxi ba ？
It doesn't matter if I wear flip-flops tomorrow, right?

沒關係 as to have no relations ##

沒關係 (méi guānxi) literally means to have no relations, we can take its literal meaning and use it to express that one thing does not relate to another. 沒關係 (méi guānxi) is used in a sentence like this:
A + 和 + B + 沒關係
和 (hé), which means and, can be replaced with other similar conjunctions like 跟 (gēn) or 與 (yǔ).
Here are some examples again:
我的私人生活跟你沒關係。
wǒ de sīrén shēnghuó gēn nǐ méi guānxi 。
My private life is none of your business.
他有沒有錢與他是不是好人沒關係。
tā yǒu méi yǒu qián yǔ tā shì bù shì hǎo rén méi guānxi 。
Whether he is rich has nothing to do with whether he is a good person.
我的女兒和那個劫匪沒任何關係。
wǒ de nǚér hé nà gè jiéfěi méi rènhé guānxi 。
My daughter has no relations with the robber at all.
