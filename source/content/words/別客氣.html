別客氣 (bié kèqi) means you are welcome or don't mention it.
你是我的好朋友，別客氣！
nǐ shì wǒ de hǎo péngyǒu ， bié kèqi ！
You are my good friend, don't mention it!
別客氣，希望能再次為您服務。
bié kèqi ， xīwàng néng zài cì wéi nín fúwù 。
You are welcome, hope to serve you again!
別客氣 (bié kèqi) literally means no formalities or don't be formal. We can take its literal meaning and use it in other situations as well. Here are some examples:
進來吧，別客氣！
jìnlái ba ， bié kèqi ！
Come in, don't be so formal!
起筷吧，別客氣！
qǐkuài ba ， bié kèqi ！
Help yourself, don't be so formal.
The synonym of 別客氣 (bié kèqi) is 不用謝 (bùyòng xiè).不用謝 (bùyòng xiè) literally means no need to thank, which is used only after someone has thanked us.
