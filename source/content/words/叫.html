We use 叫 (jiào) to express several actions done with mouth: to call, to be called, to tell and to scream. This also explains why the radical of 叫 (jiào) is 口 (kǒu), which means mouth. Let's have a look at its meanings and their usage respectively.

叫 as to call ##

叫 (jiào) is used to express calling someone's name or giving someone a name. For example: 

   

      外面有人在叫你。
      
wàimiàn yǒu rén zài jiào nǐ 。 

      
Someone is calling you outside.

   

   

      我叫她「寶貝」。
      
wǒ jiào tā 「 bǎobèi 」 。 

      
I call her 'baby'.

   

   

      不要再叫我了，我幫不到你。
      
bùyào zài jiào wǒ le ， wǒ bāng bù dào nǐ 。 

      
Stop calling me, I can't help you.

   

   

      我叫這個地方「美食天堂」。
      
wǒ jiào zhè gè dìfang 「 měishí tiāntáng 」 。 

      
I call this place 'food paradise'.

   

   
叫 as to be called ##

   叫 (jiào) means to be called as in A (someone or something) + is called + B (name). It is a commonly used word for introducing names and is very simple to use 叫 (jiào), we just put it between the subject to be named and the name given to the subject:
 subject + 叫 + name 
   

      我叫李麗。
      
wǒ jiào lǐlì 。 

      
My name is Li Li. (Lit: I am called Li Li)

   

   

      我叫「睡美人」。
      
wǒ jiào 「 shuìměirén 」 。 

      
I am called 'the sleeping beauty'.

   

   

      他叫林單，但他不會打羽毛球。
      
tā jiào líndān ， dàn tā bùhuì dǎ yǔmáoqiú 。 

      
His name is Lin Dan, but he can't play badminton.(Lit:He is called Lin Dan, but he can't play badminton.)

   

   
叫 as to tell ##

   叫 (jiào) is used when we want to tell somebody a piece of advice or to tell somebody to do something. It has to follow the structure below:
 subject + 叫 + clause 

   

      媽媽叫我多穿衣服。
      
māma jiào wǒ duō chuān yīfú 。 

      
My mother told me to wear more clothes.

   

   

      我的中文老師叫我多與當地人聊天。
      
wǒ de zhōngwén lǎoshī jiào wǒ duō yǔ dāngdìrén liáotiān 。 

      
My Chinese teacher told me to talk with the locals more often.

   

   

      我同學叫我把門關上。
      
wǒ tóngxué jiào wǒ bǎ mén guānshàng 。 

      
My classmate told me to shut the door.

   

   

      這個男人叫我把所有錢都給他。
      
zhè gè nánrén jiào wǒ bǎ suǒyǒu qián dōu gěi tā 。 

      
This man told me to give him all my money.

   

   
叫 as to scream ##

   叫 (jiào) is used when someone is saying something with a loud voice or when s/he is screaming. The following is some examples:
   

      那個孩子在叫。
      
nà gè háizi zài jiào 。 

      
The child is screaming.

   

   

      有人在叫救命！
      
yǒu rén zài jiào jiùmìng ！ 

      
Someone is screaming for help!

   

   

      她看見一隻老鼠，然後叫了起來。
      
tā kànjiàn yī zhī lǎoshǔ ， ránhòu jiào le qǐlái 。 

      
She saw a mouse and started screaming.
