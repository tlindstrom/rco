歲 to state age

subject + number + 歲

歲  is used to express age. It works the same way as “years old” in English, as in, “I am 10 years old.” However, in Chinese, a linking verb like “am” is not necessary.

我 十 歲 。
Wǒ shí suì.
I am 10 years old.
他 已經 三十 歲 了 ， 還 不懂 自己 照顧 自己 。
Tā yǐjīng sānshí suì le, hái bùdǒng zìjǐ zhàogù zìjǐ.
He is already 30 years old but still can't take care of himself.
我 快 二十五 歲 了 。
Wǒ kuài èrshíwǔ suì le.
I am turning 25 years old soon.

Asking age

To ask someone’s age, use the question word 幾.

外公 幾 歲 ？
Wàigōng jǐ suì?
How old is grandfather?
你 看起來 頗 年輕 ， 你 幾 歲 了 ？
nǐ kànqǐlái pō niánqīng ， nǐ jǐ suì le ？
You look quite young. How old are you?
