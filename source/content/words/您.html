您 means you in formal situations. The informal versions are 你 for males and 妳 for females.

For formal occasions, we use 您 as you.

您 是 黃 教授 嗎 ？
nín shì huáng jiàoshòu ma ？
Are you Professor Huang?
不好意思 ， 打擾 您 了 。
bùhǎoyìsi ， dǎrǎo nín le 。
Excuse me for disturbing you.

If you add 們 (men) after 你 (nǐ) or 您 (nín) , you get 你們 (nǐmen) or 您們 (nínmen), which means you.

您們 教 了 我 很多 東西 。
nínmen jiāo le wǒ hěnduō dōngxi 。
You taught me a lot.
謝謝 您們 ！
xièxie nínmen ！
Thank you so much!
