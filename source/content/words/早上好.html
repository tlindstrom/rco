We use 早上好 (zǎoshang hǎo) as good morning to greet people in the morning. Here are some examples:
早上好！您需要我幫忙嗎？
zǎoshàng hǎo ！ nín xūyào wǒ bāngmáng ma ？
Good morning! May I help you?
大家早上好，我是這裡新來的職員。
dàjiā zǎoshàng hǎo ， wǒ shì zhèlǐ xīn lái de zhíyuán 。
Good morning everyone, I am a new staff here.
早上好，你要咖啡還是茶？
zǎoshàng hǎo ， nǐ yào kāfēi háishì chá ？
Good morning, coffee or tea?
In casual occasions, sometimes we just say 早 (zǎo) instead of 早上好 (zǎoshàng hǎo), just like how we use morning in English.
We can also say 早安 (zǎoān) to express good morning, it is however less common than 早 (zǎo) or 早上好 (zǎoshàng hǎo) in daily conversation as 早安 (zǎoān) is more formal and often used in written form.
