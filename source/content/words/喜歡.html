喜歡 (xǐhuān) means to like. Unlike English, its sentence structure is more flexible, we can put a noun/noun phrase, a verb/verb phrase or a clause after 喜歡 (xǐhuān).
The sentence structure of 喜歡 (xǐhuān) is as the following:
subject + 喜歡 + noun/verb/clause
Let's look at how we can use this verb in detail:

喜歡 + noun/noun phrase ##

我不特別喜歡中國菜。
wǒ bù tèbié xǐhuān zhōngguó cài 。
I don't particularly like Chinese food.
我喜歡德國香腸。
wǒ xǐhuān déguó xiāngcháng 。
I love German sausages.
我喜歡紅色和藍色，但我討厭紫色。
wǒ xǐhuān hóngsè hé lánsè ， dàn wǒ tǎoyàn zǐsè 。
I love red and blue, but I hate purple.

喜歡 + verb/verb phrase ##

簡真的很喜歡看電影。
jiǎn zhēnde hěn xǐhuān kàn diànyǐng
Jane really likes to watch movies.
我不太喜歡游泳，但我喜歡玩水。
wǒ bù tài xǐhuān yóuyǒng ， dàn wǒ xǐhuān wán shuǐ 。
I don't really like to swim but I like playing with water.
我喜歡喝又甜又多奶的咖啡。
wǒ xǐhuān hē yòu tián yòu duō nǎi de kāfēi 。
I like to drink coffee with a lot of milk and sugar.

喜歡 + clause ##

我喜歡他在意見不合的時候，仍然會聽取他人的意見。
wǒ xǐhuān tā zài yìjiàn de shíhòu ， rán huì tīngqǔ tārén de yìjiàn 。
I love that when there is a disagreement, he will still listen to others' opinions.
我喜歡我的朋友跟我說實話，善意的謊言仍然是謊言。
wǒ xǐhuān wǒde péngyǒu gēn wǒ shuō shíhuà ， shànyì de huǎngyán rán shì huǎngyán 。
I like that my friends tell me the truth because white lies are still lies.
我喜歡我的朋友都很聰明，因為我可以跟他們學習很多東西。
wǒ xǐhuān wǒde péngyǒu dōu hěn cōngmíng ， yīnwèi wǒ kěyǐ gēn tāmen xuéxí hěnduō dōngxi 。
I like that all my friends are smart so I can learn a lot from them.
It's quite easy to use 喜歡 xǐhuān, right? Don't shy away from expressing what you like and let's practice! How do you translate the following sentence?
I love sex, drugs and rock'n'roll.
