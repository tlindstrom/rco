名字 (míngzi) is the Chinese equivalent of the noun name. It is one of the very first words we should learn in order to be able to introduce ourselves! Let's look at some examples:

你 叫 什麼 名字 ？
nǐ jiào shénme míngzi
What is your name?
我的 名字 叫 小花 。
wǒ de míngzi jiào xiǎohuā 。
My name is Xiao Hua.
這 個 人 的 名字 很 奇怪 。
zhè ge rén de míngzi hěn qíguài 。
This person has a weird name.

The Chinese word for full name is 姓名 (xìngmíng), which is comprised of the first character of 名字 (míngzi) with that of 姓氏 (xìngshì) , which means surname.
