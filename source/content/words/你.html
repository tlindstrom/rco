你 means you in informal occasions. The formal version is 您. 你 is used primarily for males. The female counterpart is 妳.

你 吃 了 沒有 ？
Nǐ chī le méiyǒu?
Have you eaten yet or not?
我 騙 了 你 。
Wǒ piàn le nǐ.
I lied to you.
他 討厭 你 。
Tā tǎoyàn nǐ.
He hates you.

Like all pronouns, 們 will make it plural. 你們 means you (plural) or you (plural).

你們 是 台灣人 嗎 ？
Nǐmen shì táiwānrén ma?
Are you Taiwanese?
我 會 想念 你們 。
Wǒ huì xiǎngniàn nǐmen.
I will miss you.
