Using '是...的' as an emphatic structure

This structure is commonly used to emphasize certain details of a piece of information, such as manner, time, place and people, etc. It is very similar to the English expression ‘it was...that...’
It is quite easy to incorporate this structure into a sentence, you just put 是 right before the detail you want to put the focus on and leave 的 at the end of the sentence, like this:
是 (shì) + detail to be emphasized +...+ 的 (de)
The following shows how the we move this structure within a sentence can shift its focus:
媽媽昨天是坐火車去了法國的。
māma zuótiān shì zuò huǒchē qù le fǎguó de 。
It was by train that mum went to Paris yesterday.
('How' did mum go to Paris is emphasized.)
是哥哥昨天坐了飛機去法國的。
shì gēge zuótiān zuò le fēijī qù fǎguó de 。
It was my brother that went to Paris by plane yesterday.
('Who' went to Paris is emphasized.)
媽媽是前天坐了飛機去法國的。
māma shì qiántiān zuò le fēijī qù fǎguó de 。
It was the day before yesterday that mum went to Paris by plane.
('When' did mum go to Paris is emphasized.)

Relocation of 的 from the end of the sentence

If an object is emphasized, we put 的 before 是. For example, if we want to emphasize the country that mum went to, we should write:
媽媽昨天坐了飛機去的是法國。
māma zuótiān zuò le fēijī qù de shì fǎguó 。
It was Paris that mum went to by plane yesterday.
We should not write:
媽媽昨天坐了飛機去是法國的。
māma zuótiān zuò fēijī qù shì fǎguó de 。
If an object is not emphasized in its sentence, we could put 的 either at the end of the sentence or before the object. Let's have some examples again:
我是兩年前去美國的。
wǒ shì liǎng nián qián qù měiguó de 。
It was two years ago that I went to the U.S.
To emphasize 'when' I went to the U.S, I could also write the following:
我是兩年前去的美國。
wǒ shì liǎng nián qián qù de měiguó 。
It was two years ago that I went to the U.S.
Practice time! How would you relocate 的 in the following sentence?
我是在超市裡買這些水果的。
wǒ shì zài chāoshì lǐ mǎi zhèxiē shuǐguǒ de 。
It was at the supermarket where I bought the fruit.
